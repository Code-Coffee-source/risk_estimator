Who we are

We are the student researchers from the Senior High School Department of De La Salle University-Integrated School Laguna Campus. This project is for the partial fulfillment of the requirements for our research project. We created this website to assist people all over the Philippines to estimate their risks before going outside, especially since the COVID-19 disease is still prevalent in this country.
