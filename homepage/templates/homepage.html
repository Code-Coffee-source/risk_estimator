Meet aspire.

Short for Activity Safety Planning and Infection Risk Estimator, it aims to estimate the possible risks one can have on their planned activities during the global pandemic. It is heavily based on Jimenez and Pengâ€™s estimator and the Goldberg et al. 's MyCOVIDRisk App.
In aspire you can...
Estimate Utilize premade values that are based on research to identify your COVID-19 risk when doing a planned activity.
Learn Experiment with values on-site to determine how your risk can increase or decrease based on the different circumstances given by the website.
Evaluate With this estimator, it can help you assess whether your planned activity is safe for you and your family. If not, it allows you to plan ahead of time for possible solutions regarding possible concerns.

The AGALEPA team

We are the student researchers from the Senior High School Department of De La Salle University-Integrated School Laguna Campus. We created this website to assist people all over the Philippines to estimate their risks before going outside, especially since the COVID-19 disease is still prevalent in this country. This research study is for the partial fulfillment of the requirements for our research project.
