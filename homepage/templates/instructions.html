How to use ASPIRE

Select the “Get Started” button to begin the estimation process.

You will first be met with a checklist before starting the estimation. Check the boxes once the conditions are met.

Estimating your risk will be done in only 5 steps. The progress bar will indicate which step you are in.

Step 1 is the activity location. Choose from the dropdown box what region the activity will take place.

Step 2 is the activity planned. Select from the choices of your planned activity during the pandemic.

Creating Your Own Activity. If you create your own activity, the website will ask you more questions until it understands your custom activity.

Step 2.1 is the activity level. Drag the slider to choose the intensity of your custom activity.

You can go to the advanced settings if you want more specific information to be included.

Step 2.2 is the room ventilation. Choose the nearest or similar setting for your activity.

Step 2.3 is the room size. Enter the appropriate values in the input field.

Step 3 is the time and people. Enter the appropriate values in the input fields.

Step 4 is the masks. Drag the slider to adjust the amount of people wearing masks and choose the type of mask they wear.

A summary is shown before the results. You may take this time to review and edit your results.

Step 5 is the result. You are finally done!

You can go back and test new answers.

You will answer a short survey form for the improvement and betterment of the website.
